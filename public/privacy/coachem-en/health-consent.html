Sensitive data consent (LGPD Art. 11)

Health & Wearables Consent

Version: 2.0 · Effective: 2026-05-29 · Coach'em

1. Introduction

This document is your free, informed, and unambiguous consent for processing sensitive health/sports data from wearables and Apple Health / Health Connect under Brazil’s LGPD (Art. 11).

2. Controller

V6 CORE LTDA — Coach'em · DPO: Rodrigo Antonio Lombardo Tosi · adm.ecg.19@gmail.com

3. Data covered

Aggregates only during the workout window (Start → Complete): heart rate stats, calories, distance, steps, exercise sessions. No clinical records, ECGs, diagnoses, or raw HR streams on our servers.

4. Purposes

Show metrics to you; share summaries with your linked coach for sports coaching; training history trends.

5. Legal basis

LGPD Art. 7 I and Art. 11 I — specific highlighted consent, revocable anytime.

6. Sharing

Linked coach; Firebase/Google Cloud; Apple/Google on-device APIs; authorities when legally required.

7–8. Security and retention

Encryption and access controls; retention while consent is active; no new reads after revoke; deletion on request per Privacy Policy.

9. Your rights

Access, correction, portability, deletion, withdrawal. Revoke in app (Profile → Health), iPhone Health settings, or Android Health Connect; email adm.ecg.19@gmail.com (up to 15 days response).

10. Refusal or revocation

Without consent the app works without wearable metrics. Revocation stops new collection; past summaries may remain until deletion request.

11–14. Minors, changes, declaration, contact

Guardian consent required for minors. By enabling “Connect device” in the app you confirm you read and accept this Term.
