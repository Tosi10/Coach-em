Privacy Policy - Coach'em

Last updated: 2026-04-17

This Privacy Policy explains how Coach'em ("App", "we", "our") collects, uses, and protects user information ("you").

By using Coach'em, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

1. Information we collect

1.1 Account information

  • Email and password (via Firebase Authentication) for account creation and sign-in.
  • User type (coach or athlete).

1.2 App usage data

  • Created, assigned, and completed workouts.
  • Progress information such as workout frequency, dates, feedback, and sports metrics you provide.

1.3 Technical information

  • Basic device information (model, operating system, language) for app functionality and security.

2. How we use your information

We use collected data to:

  • Enable secure sign-in and authentication.
  • Display workouts, dashboards, and performance metrics inside the app.
  • Improve user experience and build new features.
  • Keep accounts secure and prevent misuse.

3. Information sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

We may share data only:

  • With service providers necessary for app operation (for example, Firebase).
  • When required by law or by competent authorities.

In some cases, coaches may view training and progress data of athletes linked to them, as part of normal Coach'em usage.

4. Storage and security

  • Data is stored in cloud services such as Firebase/Google Cloud, using market-standard security practices.
  • We recommend using a strong password and not sharing your account with third parties.
